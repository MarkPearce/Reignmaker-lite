# 4. Urban Planning
_Your people need places to live, work, trade, and worship. Settlements are the heart of any realm._

### **Settlement Tiers**

Its assumed that settlements have **basic** equipment and services up the settlements level.

| Tier | Size       | Level Range | Max Structures |
| ---- | ---------- | ----------- | -------------- |
| 1    | Village    | 0–5         | 3              |
| 2    | Town       | 6–10        | 6              |
| 3    | City       | 11–15       | 9              |
| 4    | Metropolis | 16–20       | 12             |

> **Note:** You can't have more settlements of a higher tier than those below it. (For example, you can't have two cities and one town, but you can have two cities and two towns etc.)

### **Establish a Village** #

_Found a new community in a controlled hex, beginning your kingdom’s next chapter._

**Skill:** @Check\[type:Nature\] or @Check\[type:Survival\]

| Degree of Success | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Critical Success | Establish village with 1 [[Structures\|Structure]] |
| Success | Establish village |
| Failure | Gain 1 Unrest |
| Critical Failure | Gain 2 Unrest |

* * *

### **Upgrade a Settlement**

_Guide a village’s growth through policy, public works, or incentives to attract more settlers._

**Skill:** @Check\[type:Crafting\] or @Check\[type:Diplomacy\]

| Degree of Success | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Critical Success | Increase Level + 1 [[Structures\|Structure]] |
| Success | Increase Level |
| Failure | Gain 1 Unrest |
| Critical Failure | Gain 2 Unrest |

* * *

### **Develop a Settlement**

_Add new structures—temples, academies, markets—to enhance your cities’ capabilities._

**Skill:** @Check\[type:Crafting\] or @Check\[type:Society\]

[[Structures]]

| Degree of Success | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Critical Success | Build 2 Structures |
| Success | Build 1 Structure |
| Failure | Gain 1 Unrest |
| Critical Failure | Gain 2 Unrest |
